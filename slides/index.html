Xuan-Bach LE was a NUS undergraduate student (2007-2012) studying Double-Degree Program in Computer Science and Pure Mathematics (a list of taken courses can be found here). After receiving two first-class bachelor's degrees in 2012, he started his Computer Science PhD studies in in NUS (2013) under the President Graduate Fellowship Scheme. His supervisor is Dr. Aquinas Hobor and his mentor is Dr. Anthony W. Lin.

Since January 2018, he has taken the Research Fellow position in Prof. Jaffar Joxan's research group. He is interested in both the practical applications and theoretical foundations of Computer Science. In particular, his research topics include (but are not limited to) developing frameworks/decision procedures for program verification as well as analyzing their decidability and complexity bounds.
